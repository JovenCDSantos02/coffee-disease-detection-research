A Coffee Plant Diagnostic Tool

Welcome to Coffee Doctor, your ultimate solution for protecting and maintaining the health of your coffee plants. Coffee farming is a delicate process, where the presence of pests and diseases can significantly impact the quality and yield of your crop. Understanding the challenges faced by coffee growers, Coffee Doctor leverages advanced machine learning technology to help you quickly identify and address these issues. By simply uploading an image of your coffee plant, our system can detect common diseases and pests, providing you with accurate diagnoses and effective treatment recommendations.

Coffee Doctor, we aim to empower coffee growers by combining cutting-edge technology with practical agricultural expertise. Our platform not only identifies the specific problem affecting your plants but also offers tailored solutions to manage and cure these conditions. Whether you're dealing with fungal diseases like Leaf Rust or pests like the Mealy Bug, Coffee Doctor provides the knowledge you need to protect your coffee plants and ensure a bountiful harvest.
